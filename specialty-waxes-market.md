<p><p>Specialty waxes can be categorized into two main types: paraffin wax and synthetic wax. Paraffin wax is derived from petroleum and is commonly used in candles, cosmetics, and food packaging due to its high melting point and stability. On the other hand, synthetic waxes are man-made waxes produced from various chemicals and are often used in industries such as adhesive, coating, and pharmaceuticals. Both types of waxes play a crucial role in different applications and industries, offering unique properties and benefits.</p></p>
