<p><p>The Submarine Active Pulse Analysis System market encompasses two main types: the Passive Sonar System Type and the Active Sonar System Type. The Passive Sonar System Type is designed to detect and analyze sounds emitted by submarines, with the primary focus on listening and gathering information without alerting or transmitting any signals. On the other hand, the Active Sonar System Type emits pulses of sound waves and analyzes the echoes to determine the presence, distance, and characteristics of submarines, operating under the principle of echo location.</p></p>
