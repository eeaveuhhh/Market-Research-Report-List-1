<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fabric freshener market is divided into two main types, solid and spray. Solid fabric fresheners come in the form of blocks or beads that slowly release fragrance in the air and on fabrics over time. They are convenient for smaller spaces and provide long-lasting freshness. On the other hand, spray fabric fresheners are liquid-based products that can be directly sprayed onto fabrics to eliminate odors and leave a pleasant scent. They are widely used for quick and targeted freshness on various surfaces. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios in the market.</p></p>
