<p><p>OBD2 scanners can be classified into two main types: Generic and Enhanced. Generic scanners are basic tools that can read and clear standard OBD2 codes in most vehicles. On the other hand, Enhanced scanners offer advanced functionalities, such as accessing manufacturer-specific codes, live data, and bi-directional controls. Enhanced scanners are usually more expensive than generic ones but provide a more comprehensive diagnostic experience. Both types of scanners are widely used by mechanics, car enthusiasts, and DIYers for diagnosing and monitoring vehicle performance.</p></p>
