<p><strong>The Ultrasound Elastography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Elastography (SE)</li><li>Dynamic Elastography With Harmonic Stimuli (DEHS)</li><li>Dynamic Elastography With Transient Stimuli (DETS)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound elastography systems are used to assess tissue stiffness by measuring how it deforms under pressure. There are different types of ultrasound elastography systems in the market. Static elastography (SE) measures tissue stiffness at rest, while dynamic elastography with harmonic stimuli (DEHS) applies harmonic vibrations to the tissue. Dynamic elastography with transient stimuli (DETS) uses transient stimuli to assess tissue stiffness. Other types of elastography systems may include shear wave elastography or strain elastography, each with their own unique method for assessing tissue stiffness.</p></p>
