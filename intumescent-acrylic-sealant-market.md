<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intumescent acrylic sealants are available in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based sealants are environmentally friendly, easy to clean up, and emit less odor compared to solvent-based sealants. On the other hand, solvent-based sealants offer quicker drying times and superior adhesion to various surfaces. Both types of sealants are commonly used for firestopping applications, expanding when exposed to heat to form a protective char that helps prevent the spread of fire.</p></p>
