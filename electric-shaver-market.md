<p><p>The electric shaver market primarily consists of two main types: foil and rotary shavers. Foil shavers use oscillating blades behind a thin, perforated foil to cut hair, providing a close and precise shave. Rotary shavers, on the other hand, have circular blades that move in a circular motion to cut hair from various angles, making them ideal for cutting longer hairs and navigating contours of the face. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of consumers looking for a convenient and efficient shaving solution.</p></p>
