<p><p>High-end intraocular lenses come in different types, such as spherical lenses and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses are traditional lenses that have a uniform curved surface, providing good vision at a fixed focal length. Aspherical lenses have a more complex surface that varies in curvature, reducing optical aberrations and improving image quality. Both types of lenses can be customized to suit individual needs and provide high-quality vision correction for patients undergoing cataract surgery or refractive lens exchange.</p></p>
