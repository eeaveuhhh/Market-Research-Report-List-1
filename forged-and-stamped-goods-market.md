<p><p>Forged and stamped goods refer to metal products that are either shaped through the process of forging or stamping. Metal stamping involves the use of equipment to shape metal sheets into desired forms, while powder metallurgy part manufacturing involves shaping metal powders into parts through pressing and sintering. Custom roll forming is the process of shaping metal sheets into custom profiles through rolling. Iron and steel forging involve shaping metal through the application of heat and pressure, while nonferrous forging is the same process but using non-iron metals.</p></p>
