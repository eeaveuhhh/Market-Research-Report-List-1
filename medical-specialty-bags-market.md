<p><p>Medical specialty bags are specialized medical devices used for various purposes in the healthcare industry. Anesthesia and resuscitation bags are used for delivering anesthetic gases or delivering air or oxygen during resuscitation. Ostomy drainage bags collect waste from surgically created openings in the body. IV fluid bags are used for delivering intravenous fluids. CAPD bags are used in continuous ambulatory peritoneal dialysis. Blood collection bags collect and store blood samples. Urine collection bags collect and store urine samples. Enteral feeding bags are used for delivering liquid nutrition directly into the stomach or intestine.</p></p>
