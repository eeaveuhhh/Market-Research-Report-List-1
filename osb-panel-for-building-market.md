<p><p>OSB panels come in four different market types: OSB/1, OSB/2, OSB/3, and OSB/4. OSB/1 is suitable for interior use where it won't be exposed to moisture. OSB/2 can be used for general construction purposes. OSB/3 is designed for structural applications where high moisture resistance is required. OSB/4 is the most heavy-duty option, suitable for use in high-moisture environments or where high load-bearing capacity is needed. Each market type varies in terms of strength, durability, and moisture resistance.</p></p>
