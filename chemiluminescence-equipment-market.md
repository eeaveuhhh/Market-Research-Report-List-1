<p><p>Chemiluminescence equipment is used for detecting the presence of specific substances through the emission of light. The different types of chemiluminescence equipment include Direct Luminescence, Enzymatic Luminescence, and Electrochemiluminescence. Direct Luminescence utilizes a directly excited molecule to emit light, Enzymatic Luminescence involves enzyme-catalyzed reactions to produce light, and Electrochemiluminescence uses an electrically generated reaction to emit light. Each type of chemiluminescence equipment has its own unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
