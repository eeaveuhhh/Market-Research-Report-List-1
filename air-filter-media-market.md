<p><p>Air filter media market consists of three main types: HEPA grade media, MERV grade media, and ULPA grade media. HEPA grade media provides the highest level of filtration, capturing particles as small as 0.3 microns. MERV grade media offers efficient filtration for residential and commercial applications, capturing particles between 0.3 to 10 microns. ULPA grade media provides the highest level of filtration efficiency, capturing particles as small as 0.12 microns. Each type of media caters to specific filtration needs based on particle size and level of filtration required.</p></p>
