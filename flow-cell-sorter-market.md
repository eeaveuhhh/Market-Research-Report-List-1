<p><p>Flow Cell Sorter market includes two main types: Fluorescent-activated Flow Cell Sorter and Magnetic-activated Flow Cell Sorter. The Fluorescent-activated Flow Cell Sorter market utilizes fluorescent labeling to sort cells based on specific characteristics such as size, shape, and marker expression. On the other hand, the Magnetic-activated Flow Cell Sorter market separates cells using magnetic beads conjugated to specific antibodies. Both types of sorters are essential tools in various fields including research, clinical diagnostics, and drug discovery.</p></p>
