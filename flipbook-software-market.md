<p><p>The flipbook software market consists of two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise flipbook software requires installation and runs directly on a user's personal computer or server. It offers more control over data security and customization but may require IT support for maintenance. Cloud-based flipbook software is accessed through the internet and hosted on remote servers. It offers convenience, scalability, and regular updates but may raise concerns about data privacy and internet access. Both types cater to individuals and businesses seeking to create interactive digital flipbooks.</p></p>
