<p><strong>The Food Shelf Life Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Real Time</li><li>Accelerated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food shelf life testing is conducted to determine the durability and quality of food products. There are two main types of testing: real-time and accelerated market testing. Real-time testing involves storing the product at recommended conditions and monitoring its quality over a specific period, typically the expected shelf life. This provides insight into the product's behavior over time. Accelerated market testing, on the other hand, involves subjecting the product to exaggerated conditions such as high temperature or humidity to hasten the quality deterioration process. This helps to estimate the shelf life of the product more quickly in order to meet market demands.</p></p>
