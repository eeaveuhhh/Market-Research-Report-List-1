<p><p>Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) Devices are used to treat sleep apnea by delivering continuous pressured air to keep the airways open. There are three main types of PAP devices in the market. AutoCPAP (APAP) devices automatically adjust the pressure based on individual needs throughout the night, providing flexibility and comfort. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices deliver a constant, prescribed pressure during both inhalation and exhalation. Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BPAP) devices provide different pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, suitable for individuals who require different air pressure support.</p></p>
