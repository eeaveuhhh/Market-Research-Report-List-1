<p><strong>The Fiberglass Reinforced Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Molding Type</li><li>Extrusion Molding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass Reinforced Grating market includes Compression Molding Type and Extrusion Molding Type products. The Compression Molding Type offers high strength and durability through a process of combining resin and fiberglass under high pressure, making it suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. On the other hand, Extrusion Molding Type utilizes a continuous process of pulling molten resin and fiberglass through a shaping die, offering a lightweight and cost-effective solution for applications requiring corrosion resistance and easy installation.</p></p>
