<p><p>Mineral screening is the process of separating minerals based on their size and other physical properties. Manufacturers are focusing on developing innovative screening machines and equipment to cater to the specific needs of different minerals and applications. For instance, vibrating screens with high-frequency vibrations are widely used for fine screening of minerals.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the integration of automation and smart technologies in screening processes. Automation helps in achieving higher productivity, reducing labor costs, and ensuring consistent screening performance.
