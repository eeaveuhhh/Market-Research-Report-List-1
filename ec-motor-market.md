<p><strong>The EC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EC motors are commonly divided into two types, inner rotor motors and outer rotor motors. Inner rotor motors have the stator on the outside and the rotor on the inside, while outer rotor motors have the opposite configuration with the rotor on the outside and the stator on the inside. Inner rotor motors are more compact and are often used in applications where space is limited, while outer rotor motors are larger and provide higher torque, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
