<p><p>Automatic Identification Systems (AIS) have two main classes in the market - AIS Class A and AIS Class B. AIS Class A systems are mandated for all vessels over 300 gross tons, providing real-time information on vessel position, speed, course, and other essential data to enhance navigation safety. AIS Class B systems are typically used by smaller vessels and provide similar information but with less frequent updates. Both systems help prevent collisions and improve communication between vessels, ultimately increasing overall maritime safety.</p></p>
