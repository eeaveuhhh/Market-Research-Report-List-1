<p><strong>The Black Foam Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Black foam glass is available in two main market types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass is made up of sealed cells filled with gas, providing excellent insulation properties. It is commonly used in applications where moisture resistance and strength are important. In contrast, open cell foam glass has interconnected cells allowing air and moisture to pass through, making it more breathable but less dense. It is typically used in applications where air circulation or drainage is necessary.</p></p>
