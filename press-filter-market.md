<p><p>A press filter, also known as a filter press, is a device used for solid-liquid separation in various industries such as mining, chemical, pharmaceutical, and food and beverage. It consists of a series of vertically arranged filter plates with filter cloth in between, supported by a framework. The press filter is used to remove solids from liquid by applying pressure and forcing the liquid through the filter cloth, leaving the solid particles trapped in the filter.</p><p>The press filter market has been experiencing substantial growth in recent years and is projected to continue growing at a significant rate during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving the market growth is the increasing demand for press filters in wastewater treatment plants and industries that generate high-volume liquid waste.
