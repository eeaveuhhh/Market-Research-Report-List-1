<p><strong>The Car Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car wrap films are available in two main types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is made by pouring liquid vinyl onto a casting sheet, resulting in a thin and highly flexible film that is ideal for complex curves and contours. Calendered film, on the other hand, is made by passing the vinyl through a series of rollers, resulting in a thicker and less flexible film. Cast film is typically more durable and offers better conformability, while calendered film is more cost-effective and easier to install.</p></p>
