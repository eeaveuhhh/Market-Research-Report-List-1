<p><p>Electric ferries are categorized into two main types: pure electric ferries and hybrid ferries. Pure electric ferries are powered solely by electric energy, either from shore charging or on-board batteries. On the other hand, hybrid ferries combine electric propulsion with traditional fuel engines, allowing for greater flexibility and increased range. Both types of electric ferries are gaining popularity due to their environmental benefits, reduced operating costs, and compliance with emissions regulations.</p></p>
