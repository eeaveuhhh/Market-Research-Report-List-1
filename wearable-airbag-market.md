<p><p>The wearable airbag market includes two main types: airbag belt and airbag jack. Airbag belts are wearable devices that can be attached around the waist and inflate in case of a fall or collision, providing protection to the user's vital organs and lower back. On the other hand, airbag jacks are portable safety devices used for lifting heavy objects or vehicles off the ground. They utilize airbag technology to provide a cushioned lifting mechanism, ensuring safety and preventing injuries during lifting operations. Both these wearable airbag types aim to enhance safety and minimize the risk of accidents in specific scenarios.</p></p>
