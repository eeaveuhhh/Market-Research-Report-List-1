<p><p>AMOLED Fine Metal Mask Market has three main types: Etching, Electroforming, and Multi Material Composite Method. Etching involves removing parts of metal using chemical processes to create the mask. Electroforming is a method of depositing metal onto a substrate to form the mask. Multi Material Composite Method combines different materials to fabricate the mask. Each of these methods has its own advantages and applications in the AMOLED industry, catering to different needs and requirements of manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
