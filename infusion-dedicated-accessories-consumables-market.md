<p><p>The infusion dedicated accessories & consumables market consists of various types such as volumetric infusion pumps, insulin infusion pumps, enteral infusion pumps, syringe infusion pumps, ambulatory infusion pumps, PCA pumps, and implantable infusion pumps. Volumetric infusion pumps are used for delivering large volumes of fluids, while insulin infusion pumps are specifically designed for administering insulin. Enteral infusion pumps are used for delivering nutrition directly into the stomach or intestines. Syringe infusion pumps utilize syringes to deliver medication. Ambulatory infusion pumps are portable devices for continuous medication delivery. PCA pumps allow patients to self-administer pain medication. Implantable infusion pumps are surgically implanted devices for long-term drug delivery.</p></p>
