<p><strong>The Ammonium Paratungstate（APT） Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neutralization</li><li>Tungsten Acid Method</li><li>Thermal Decomposition Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ammonium Paratungstate (APT) market is categorized into three types based on production methods: neutralization, tungsten acid method, and thermal decomposition method. In the neutralization method, tungsten ores are dissolved in an ammonia solution and precipitated as APT. Tungsten acid method involves the reaction of tungsten oxides with hydrochloric acid to form APT. Thermal decomposition method includes heating ammonium metatungstate at high temperatures to produce APT. Each method offers unique advantages and is used in different industrial applications.</p></p>
