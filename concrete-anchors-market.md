<p><ul><li>Mechanical Concrete Anchors</li><li>Adhesive Concrete Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete anchors market consists of two main types: mechanical concrete anchors and adhesive concrete anchors. Mechanical concrete anchors are designed to hold objects in place by expanding and gripping the concrete tightly. They rely on friction and mechanical interlock for their strength. On the other hand, adhesive concrete anchors use chemical adhesives to bond the anchor to the concrete surface. They create a strong and durable connection by filling gaps and bonding the anchor to the concrete. Both types offer reliable solutions for securing objects to concrete surfaces.</p></p>
