<p><p>A golf cart is a small vehicle designed for short-distance transportation on golf courses. It is electrically powered and usually has a seating capacity of two to four people. In recent years, golf carts have become popular not just in golf courses but also as a mode of transportation in residential communities, resorts, and commercial areas. </p><p>On the other hand, a neighborhood electric vehicle (NEV) is a type of low-speed electric vehicle typically used for short-distance transportation within communities. Similar to a golf cart, NEVs are also battery-powered and have a limited seating capacity of two to four people. NEVs are designed for use in neighborhoods, parks, and other low-speed environments.</p><p>The golf cart and NEV market have witnessed significant growth in recent years. The increasing popularity of golf as a recreational sport, coupled with the rising trend of gated communities and resorts, has boosted the demand for golf carts. Additionally, there is a growing trend of customization, with customers opting for personalized design and accessories for their golf carts and NEVs.</p><p>Overall, the golf cart and NEV market are expected to witness robust growth in the coming years, driven by the increasing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable transportation solutions in both residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
