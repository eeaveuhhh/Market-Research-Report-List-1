<p><p>X-Ray Crystallography Instruments market consists of two main types: X-Ray Powder Diffraction and Single-crystal Xrd. X-Ray Powder Diffraction instruments are used to analyze crystalline materials in powder form, providing information on crystal structure and composition. Single-crystal Xrd instruments, on the other hand, are specifically designed to study the atomic arrangement of a single crystal sample, offering detailed insights into symmetry and bonding within the crystal lattice. Both types of instruments play a crucial role in various scientific fields, including material science, chemistry, and biology.</p></p>
