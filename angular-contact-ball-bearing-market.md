<p><p>Angular contact ball bearings are classified based on the contact angle between the inner and outer raceways. Other markets may also feature varying contact angles outside of the standard range, such as 50° or 60°, to cater to specific application requirements. Choosing the right contact angle is crucial in achieving optimal performance and efficiency in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
