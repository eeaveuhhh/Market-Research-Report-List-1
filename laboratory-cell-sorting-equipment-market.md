<p><strong>The Laboratory Cell Sorting Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorting</li><li>Magnetic-activated Cell Sorting</li><li>MEMS - Microfluidics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory cell sorting equipment market includes various types of technologies used for sorting cells based on specific characteristics. Fluorescent-activated cell sorting utilizes fluorescent labeling to identify and sort cells. Magnetic-activated cell sorting separates cells using magnetic beads attached to specific cell surface markers. MEMS - Microfluidics market incorporates microfluidic channels to sort cells based on size, shape, or other physical properties. These technologies are essential tools in research, diagnostics, and therapeutic applications in fields such as cancer research, immunology, and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
