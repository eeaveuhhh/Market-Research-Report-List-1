<p><p>5G Femto Base Station market includes two main types: Stand Alone (SA) and Non-Stand Alone (NSA). SA 5G Femto Base Stations operate independently without the need for existing LTE networks, offering higher speeds and lower latency. On the other hand, NSA 5G Femto Base Stations rely on LTE networks for initial deployment, making them a more cost-effective option. Both types cater to different market needs, with SA focusing on performance and NSA providing a more economical solution for operators.</p></p>
