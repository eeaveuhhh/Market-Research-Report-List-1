<p><p>The 3D Diagnostic Imaging Services market consists of various types including ultrasound, MRI, X-ray, computed tomography, and others. Ultrasound employs sound waves to create images of organs and tissues inside the body. MRI uses strong magnets and radio waves to generate detailed images of the body's internal structures. X-ray utilizes electromagnetic radiation to produce images of bones and certain tissues. Computed tomography combines X-rays and computer technology to create cross-sectional images of the body. The "others" category includes emerging imaging technologies such as positron emission tomography (PET) and single-photon emission computed tomography (SPECT).</p></p>
