<p><p>Film packaging materials are classified into two main types - rigid and flexible. Rigid film packaging materials are typically made of materials like glass, metal, or rigid plastics, providing a sturdy and protective packaging solution for products. On the other hand, flexible film packaging materials are made of materials like plastic films, paper, or aluminum foil, offering a lightweight and adaptable packaging solution that can conform to the shape of the product. Both types of film packaging materials offer different benefits and are used in various industries.</p></p>
