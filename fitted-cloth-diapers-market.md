<p><p>Fitted cloth diapers come in two main types: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer diapers consist of one layer of fabric, which can provide a more trim fit but may require additional boosters for absorbency. Multi-layer diapers, on the other hand, have multiple layers of fabric sewn together to provide greater absorbency without the need for additional inserts. Both types offer different options for parents based on their preferences for fit and absorbency levels.</p></p>
