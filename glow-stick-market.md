<p><p>A glow stick is a self-contained, short-term light source that consists of a translucent plastic tube filled with a chemical solution. When the tube is bent, the inner glass vial breaks, allowing the chemicals to mix and produce a glowing light through chemiluminescence. Glow sticks are popular for various recreational, decorative, and emergency purposes due to their portable, non-toxic, and long-lasting illumination.</p><p>The Glow Stick Market is experiencing significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include increased popularity and adoption of glow sticks in entertainment events, festivals, and concerts, as well as a growing demand for unique light sources in the party and decoration industry. Additionally, the rise in outdoor activities such as camping, hiking, and night-time sports is contributing to the increased sales of glow sticks for safety and visibility purposes.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Glow Stick Market is the introduction of eco-friendly and reusable glow sticks that reduce waste and environmental impact. Overall, the market is expected to continue expanding as glow sticks remain a popular and versatile lighting solution for various occasions.</p></p>
