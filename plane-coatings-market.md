<p><p>Plane coatings market is segmented into liquid coating and powder coating types. Liquid coatings are typically solvent-based or water-based coatings that are applied using a spray gun or brush. They provide a smooth finish and are often used for intricate designs. Powder coatings, on the other hand, are applied as a dry powder and then melted to form a protective layer. They are known for their durability and resistance to corrosion. Both liquid and powder coatings cater to different needs and preferences in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
