<p><strong>The Barrier Bottle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-Layers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barrier bottle market can be categorized into two primary types: single layer and multi-layers. Single layer bottles are made from a single material and provide basic protection against light, oxygen, and moisture. On the other hand, multi-layer bottles consist of different layers of materials such as PET, PE, and EVOH, offering enhanced barrier properties for better protection of the contents from external factors. Multi-layer bottles are gaining popularity due to their superior barrier capabilities compared to single layer bottles.</p></p>
