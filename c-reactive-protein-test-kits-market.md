<p><strong>The C-Reactive Protein Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunoturbidimetric</li><li>ELISA</li><li>CLIA</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>C-Reactive Protein (CRP) Test Kits market consists of various types like Immunoturbidimetric, Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA), and others. Immunoturbidimetric kits rely on the principle of measuring the change in turbidity of a solution after binding with CRP. ELISA kits use antibodies to detect CRP levels, while CLIA kits use light emissions to quantify CRP. Other kits may include rapid tests or point-of-care devices for quick and easy CRP testing. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of sensitivity, specificity, and convenience for CRP measurement.</p></p>
