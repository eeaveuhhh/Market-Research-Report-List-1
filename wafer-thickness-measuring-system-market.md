Step Profiler system measures thickness by scanning the wafer surface with a stylus or optical beam. Ellipsometer system measures thickness by analyzing changes in polarization of light reflected off the wafer surface. Other systems may use different techniques such as interferometry or acoustic waves to measure wafer thickness. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the application and precision requirements of the user.</p></p>
