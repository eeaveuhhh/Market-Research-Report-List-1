<p><p>Fluid conveyance systems are essential for transferring fluids within various industries. There are three main types of fluid conveyance systems based on the medium they use: fuel, air, and hydraulic. Fuel-based systems are commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries for transporting gasoline and diesel. Air-based systems are utilized in pneumatic applications for conveying compressed air. Hydraulic-based systems are widely used in heavy machinery and construction equipment for transmitting hydraulic fluids to power different components. Each type serves specific purposes in different industries.</p></p>
