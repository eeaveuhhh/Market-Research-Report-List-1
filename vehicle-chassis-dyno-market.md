<p><ul><li>Multi Roller</li><li>Single Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Chassis Dyno market includes two main types: Multi Roller and Single Roller dynos. Multi Roller dynos use multiple rollers to simulate real-world driving conditions, providing more accurate and consistent results. Single Roller dynos, on the other hand, use a single roller to measure the performance of a vehicle's engine. While Multi Roller dynos are typically more expensive and complex, they offer higher precision and versatility. Single Roller dynos are simpler and more affordable, making them a popular choice for smaller facilities or budget-conscious consumers.</p></p>
