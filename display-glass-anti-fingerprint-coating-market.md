<p><p>Display glass anti-fingerprint coatings are categorized into two main types: Fluorine Modified Organic Groups and Perfluoropolyether Group. Fluorine Modified Organic Groups coatings are chemically modified with fluorine atoms, which provide water and oil repellent properties to the glass surface. On the other hand, Perfluoropolyether Group coatings contain fluorine atoms and have high thermal stability, making them ideal for high temperature applications. Both types of coatings effectively prevent smudging and fingerprints on display glass surfaces, enhancing the overall user experience.</p></p>
