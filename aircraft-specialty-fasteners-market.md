<p><p>Threaded Fasteners in the Aircraft Specialty Fasteners market refer to fasteners that have threads on them, such as bolts, screws, and nuts. These fasteners are used to hold components together in aircrafts. On the other hand, Non-Threaded Fasteners include rivets, pins, and clips which do not have any threading. They are also used in aircraft construction for joining materials together. Both Threaded and Non-Threaded Fasteners play a crucial role in maintaining the structural integrity of aircrafts.</p></p>
