<p><p>Synthetic Abrasive Market Types comprise of Bonded Abrasive, Coated Abrasive, and Super Abrasive. Bonded Abrasives are materials like grinding wheels which are firmly held together by a binding agent. Coated Abrasives include sandpaper which have abrasive material coated onto a backing material. Super Abrasives refer to diamond or cubic boron nitride abrasives which are used for precision grinding processes. Each of these types serves different purposes and industries, providing a wide range of options for various abrasive applications.</p></p>
