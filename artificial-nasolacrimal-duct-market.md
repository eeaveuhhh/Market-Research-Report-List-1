<p><p>The artificial nasolacrimal duct market includes various types of devices to treat lacrimal duct obstructions, such as mono canalicular stents and canalicular stents. Mono canalicular stents are designed to be inserted into one of the tear ducts to keep it open and allow tears to drain properly. Canalicular stents, on the other hand, are placed in both tear ducts to restore proper drainage. Both types of stents are used to alleviate symptoms of tearing, discharge, and infection caused by blocked tear ducts.</p></p>
