<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Balancing Weight Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clip-On Type</li><li>Adhesive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive wheel balancing weight market offers two main types: Clip-On Type and Adhesive Type. Clip-On Type weights are attached to the rim of the wheel by clipping onto the edge, providing a secure hold. Adhesive Type weights, on the other hand, are affixed to the wheel using a strong adhesive backing, ensuring a more permanent attachment. Both types serve the same purpose of balancing the wheel by compensating for any weight imbalances, ensuring a smooth and safe driving experience.</p></p>
