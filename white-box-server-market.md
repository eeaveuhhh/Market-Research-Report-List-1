<p><ul><li>Rack-mount Server</li><li>Blade Server</li><li>Whole Cabinet Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>White box servers are typically categorized into three main types based on their form factor: rack-mount servers, blade servers, and whole cabinet servers. Rack-mount servers are compact and easily stacked in server racks, allowing for efficient use of space. Blade servers are even more compact and are designed to be easily inserted and removed from a chassis, making them ideal for data centers with limited space. Whole cabinet servers are larger and designed to handle heavy workloads, often used for high-performance computing and storage applications.</p></p>
