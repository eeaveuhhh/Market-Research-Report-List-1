<p><strong>The Galvanized Sheet for Construction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro Galvanized Sheet</li><li>Hot Dip Galvanized Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized sheets are commonly used in construction for their corrosion-resistant properties. There are two main types in the market: Electro Galvanized Sheet and Hot Dip Galvanized Sheet. Electro Galvanized Sheets are coated with zinc using an electrolytic process, while Hot Dip Galvanized Sheets are immersed in a bath of molten zinc. Electro Galvanized Sheets are ideal for indoor applications, while Hot Dip Galvanized Sheets are preferred for outdoor use due to their thicker coating. Both types provide excellent protection against rust and are widely used in construction projects.</p></p>
