<p><p>The Glaucoma Treatment Devices Market includes two main types: Glaucoma Drainage Devices and Glaucoma Laser Devices. Glaucoma Drainage Devices are implantable devices that help to regulate the flow of aqueous humor, reducing intraocular pressure. They are typically used when medication or laser treatment has failed. Glaucoma Laser Devices, on the other hand, use laser technology to treat glaucoma by improving the drainage of fluid from the eye or reducing the production of fluid. Both types of devices aim to manage and control the progression of glaucoma.</p></p>
