<p><ul><li>Polymeric Water Ultrafiltration Machine</li><li>Ceramic Water Ultrafiltration Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water ultrafiltration machines are categorized based on the materials used for filtration membranes. Polymeric water ultrafiltration machines use synthetic polymeric membranes that offer high efficiency and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, ceramic water ultrafiltration machines use durable ceramic membranes that are resistant to chemical and mechanical damage. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as wastewater treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals to remove contaminants and purify water for various applications.</p></p>
