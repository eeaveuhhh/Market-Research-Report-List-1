<p><p>Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen (CRMB) is a type of bitumen mixed with recycled rubber to enhance its properties. The two main methods of incorporating crumb rubber into bitumen are the Wet Method and Dry Method. The Wet Method involves blending hot bitumen with rubber granules, while the Dry Method involves adding rubber powder to the bitumen. Both methods provide improved elasticity, durability, and resistance to cracking, making CRMB suitable for various road construction applications. Each method has its own advantages and considerations for implementation.</p></p>
