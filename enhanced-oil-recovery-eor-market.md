<p><p>Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) refers to various techniques used to extract more oil from reservoirs beyond primary and secondary methods. Gas injection involves injecting gases like CO2 or natural gas to displace oil and increase production. Thermal injection utilizes steam or hot water to reduce oil viscosity and improve flow. Chemical injection involves injecting chemicals to alter rock properties or reduce oil viscosity. Each EOR method has its own advantages and is chosen based on reservoir conditions and economic factors.</p></p>
