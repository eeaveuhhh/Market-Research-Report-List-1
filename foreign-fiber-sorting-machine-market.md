<p><p>Foreign fiber sorting machines are classified into different types based on their working principle. Blowing-Carding machines use a combination of blowing and carding techniques to sort fibers. Single Process machines perform sorting in a single step without the need for multiple stages. Air Spinning machines use air to separate fibers based on their different properties. These different types cater to various needs and preferences in the foreign fiber sorting machine market.</p></p>
