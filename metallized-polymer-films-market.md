<p><p>Metallized PET Film and Metallized OPP Film are two types of metallized polymer films used in various industries. Metallized PET Film, made from polyethylene terephthalate, offers excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light, making it ideal for packaging applications. On the other hand, Metallized OPP Film, made from oriented polypropylene, is known for its high tensile strength and durability, making it suitable for applications such as gift wrapping, labels, and insulation. Both types of films provide a cost-effective solution for enhancing product appearance and protection.</p></p>
