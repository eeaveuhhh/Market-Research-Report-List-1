<p><p>External fixator devices are medical tools used to stabilize and immobilize fractured bones or injured body parts during the healing process. These devices are made up of metal pins or wires that are inserted into the bone on both sides of the fracture, and an external frame that holds the pins in place.</p><p>The market for external fixator devices is experiencing significant growth, mainly driven by the increasing incidence of fractures and sports injuries worldwide. Additionally, the rise in geriatric population and the growing prevalence of road accidents are also contributing to the market growth.
