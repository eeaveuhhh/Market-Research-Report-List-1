<p><p>E-pick systems are tools used in warehouses to streamline order picking processes. Manual e-pick systems require workers to manually pick items from storage locations based on instruction from the system. Auto guided e-pick systems use automated robots or vehicles to move around the warehouse and pick items as directed by the system. Both types of e-pick systems aim to increase efficiency and accuracy in the order picking process, but auto guided systems can offer more speed and precision.</p></p>
