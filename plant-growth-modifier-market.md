<p><p>Plant Growth Modifiers are classified into different types based on their mode of action. Auxins are hormones that promote cell elongation and root development. Cytokinins stimulate cell division and are involved in leaf senescence. Gibberellins are responsible for stem elongation and seed germination. Other plant growth modifiers include abscisic acid, ethylene, and brassinosteroids, which regulate various aspects of plant growth and development. Each type of plant growth modifier plays a crucial role in promoting healthy plant growth and improving crop yields.</p></p>
