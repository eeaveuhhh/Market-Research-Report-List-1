<p><p>Cotton pulp market can be categorized into two main types: bleached and unbleached. Bleached cotton pulp undergoes a process where chemicals are used to remove impurities and achieve a bright white color. This type of pulp is commonly used in products where a clean and smooth finish is desired, such as in the production of high-quality paper or textiles. On the other hand, unbleached cotton pulp retains its natural color and is typically used in products where a more organic or rustic look is preferred.</p></p>
