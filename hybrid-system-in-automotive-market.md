<p><p>In the automotive market, hybrid systems refer to the combination of traditional internal combustion engines with electric propulsion. Start-stop technology automatically shuts off the engine when the vehicle is stationary, reducing fuel consumption and emissions. Regenerative braking converts kinetic energy into electrical energy to recharge the hybrid battery during deceleration. EV drive allows the vehicle to operate solely on electric power for a certain distance or speed, reducing dependence on fossil fuels. These technologies contribute to improved fuel efficiency, reduced environmental impact, and enhanced driving experience.</p></p>
