<p><p>Voice recognition for the automotive market can be categorized into three main types: embedded, non-embedded, and others. Non-embedded voice recognition, on the other hand, relies on external devices such as smartphones or wearables to process voice commands. The "others" category includes alternative voice recognition methods, such as cloud-based systems that utilize the internet to provide voice control functionalities.</p></p>
