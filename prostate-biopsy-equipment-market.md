<p><p>Prostate biopsy equipment is used for the diagnosis and treatment of prostate cancer, a prevalent form of cancer affecting men worldwide. The equipment is designed to obtain tissue samples from the prostate gland for further analysis. Biopsies are typically performed using two methods: transrectal ultrasound (TRUS) and transperineal.</p><p>The transrectal ultrasound method involves inserting an ultrasound probe into the rectum to visualize the prostate gland and guide the biopsy needle. Meanwhile, the transperineal method involves accessing the prostate through the perineum, the area between the scrotum and anus. This method has gained popularity due to greater accuracy and reduced risk of infection.</p><p>The market for prostate biopsy equipment is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
