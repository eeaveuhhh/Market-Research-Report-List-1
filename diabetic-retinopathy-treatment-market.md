<p><strong>The Diabetic Retinopathy Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (PDR)</li><li>Non-Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (NPDR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diabetic retinopathy treatment market is divided into two major types: Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (PDR) and Non-Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (NPDR). PDR is an advanced stage characterized by the formation of abnormal blood vessels in the back of the eye and is treated with laser surgery, injections, or vitrectomy. In contrast, NPDR is an earlier stage that involves damaged blood vessels in the retina, which can be managed by controlling blood sugar levels, regular eye exams, and prescribed medications. Both types of diabetic retinopathy require proper treatment to prevent vision loss.</p></p>
