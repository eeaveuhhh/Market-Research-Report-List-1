<p><p>Wind turbine gear lubricants are essential for the smooth operation and longevity of wind turbines. There are two main types of lubricants used in the market: mineral-based and synthetic-based. Mineral-based lubricants are derived from crude oil and offer good lubrication properties at a lower cost.
