<p><ul><li>Chemical Sunscreens</li><li>Physical Sunscreens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical sunscreens use chemical filters to absorb UV radiation and convert it into heat, while physical sunscreens use mineral filters like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide to create a physical barrier that reflects and scatters UV rays. Chemical sunscreens are popular for their lightweight feel and easy application, while physical sunscreens are favored for their broad-spectrum protection and suitability for sensitive skin. Both types of sunscreens are widely available in the market, catering to different preferences and skin types.</p></p>
