<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Auto Guided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital picking systems can be categorized into two main types: manual and auto-guided. Manual picking systems involve human workers physically picking items from shelves based on instructions from a digital system. Auto-guided picking systems use automated machines, such as robots or conveyor belts, to pick and transport items based on digital commands without human intervention. Both types of systems aim to streamline the picking process in warehouses and distribution centers, but auto-guided systems offer higher efficiency and accuracy compared to manual systems.</p></p>
