<p><strong>The Twin-Screw Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sealed Twin-Screw Pump</li><li>Non-sealed Twin-Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Twin-Screw Pumps market comprises two main types: Sealed Twin-Screw Pumps and Non-sealed Twin-Screw Pumps. Sealed Twin-Screw Pumps are designed with a sealing arrangement that prevents any leakage of fluid during operation. These pumps are commonly used in applications where the pumped fluid needs to be contained and isolated. On the other hand, Non-sealed Twin-Screw Pumps do not have a sealing arrangement, allowing some level of fluid leakage during operation. These pumps are often used in applications where leakage is permissible or desirable, such as lubrication systems or certain chemical processes.</p></p>
