<p><p>Copper plating electrolyte and additives are chemicals used in the process of copper plating, which involves depositing a layer of copper onto a substrate. One of the key drivers of market growth is the increasing demand for copper plating in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace. Copper plating is used for its excellent electrical conductivity and corrosion resistance properties, making it an essential process in the production of printed circuit boards, connectors, and other electronic components.</p><p>Moreover, the growing trend of miniaturization in electronics and the need for high-performance electronic devices are also driving the demand for copper plating electrolyte and additives.
