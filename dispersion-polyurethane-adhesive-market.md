<p><p>Dispersion polyurethane adhesive is a versatile adhesive used in various industries. Thermoset polyurethane adhesives are cured through heat or chemical reactions, providing excellent durability and high temperature resistance. On the other hand, thermoplastic polyurethane adhesives can be melted and reformed multiple times, making them suitable for applications where flexibility is required. Both types offer strong bonding properties and are widely used in construction, automotive, and packaging industries for their reliability and superior performance.</p></p>
