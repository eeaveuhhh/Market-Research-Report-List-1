<p><p>Horizontal hydraulic pipe benders are divided into three main types based on their automation level: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual pipe benders require operators to manually control all bending functions, making them more labor-intensive but also more affordable. Semi-automatic pipe benders have partially automated features that assist operators in bending pipes more efficiently. Fully automatic pipe benders are the most advanced, with automated controls for the entire bending process, resulting in higher productivity and precision, but also a higher cost.</p></p>
