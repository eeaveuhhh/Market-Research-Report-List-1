<p><strong>The Unbleached Hardwood Kraft Pulp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Pulp</li><li>Semi-Chemical Pulp</li><li>Mechanical Pulp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unbleached Hardwood Kraft Pulp is primarily categorized into three market types - Chemical Pulp, Semi-Chemical Pulp, and Mechanical Pulp. Chemical Pulp is produced using chemicals to break down lignin in the wood fibers, resulting in high-quality pulp suitable for various paper products. Semi-Chemical Pulp involves a partial chemical treatment of wood fibers, offering a balance between strength and cost-effectiveness. Mechanical Pulp, on the other hand, relies on mechanical processes to separate fibers, producing a lower quality pulp used for newsprint and packaging materials.</p></p>
