<p><p>Sodium silicate powder is available in two main types: hydrous and anhydrous. Hydrous sodium silicate contains water in its chemical structure, making it more stable and easier to handle in certain applications. On the other hand, anhydrous sodium silicate is water-free and offers higher concentration and purity for specialized industrial uses. Both types are widely used in diverse sectors such as detergents, adhesives, food processing, construction, and automotive industries.</p></p>
