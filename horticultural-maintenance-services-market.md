<p><p>Horticultural maintenance services encompass two main market types: corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves addressing immediate issues such as damaged plants or equipment, pruning overgrown areas, or fixing irrigation problems. On the other hand, preventive maintenance focuses on proactive measures to maintain the health and aesthetics of outdoor spaces, including regular pruning, fertilizing, pest and disease control, soil testing, and general upkeep. Both market types aim to enhance and sustain the overall appearance and functionality of horticultural landscapes.</p></p>
