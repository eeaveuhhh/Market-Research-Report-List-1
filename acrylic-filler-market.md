<p><p>Acrylic fillers can be classified into two main types: One-Component Acrylic Filler and Two-Component Acrylic Filler. One-Component Acrylic Fillers come ready to use and require no additional mixing, making them convenient and easy to apply. Two-Component Acrylic Fillers, on the other hand, consist of two separate components that need to be mixed together before application. These fillers offer a higher level of customization and are often preferred for more demanding applications where durability and strength are key factors.</p></p>
