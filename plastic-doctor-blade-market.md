<p><p>The plastic doctor blade market finds its application in various printing techniques such as flexo, gravure, and offset printing. The flexo printing technique utilizes doctor blades made of plastic to remove excess ink from the printing cylinder. Similarly, in gravure printing, plastic doctor blades are used to clean the engraved cells on the cylinder, ensuring precise ink transfer. In offset printing, plastic doctor blades help in removing surplus water and unwanted ink from the plate. Additionally, plastic doctor blades find use in other sectors requiring precision cleaning or scraping, such as in the cleaning of conveyor belts or removing excess material in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
