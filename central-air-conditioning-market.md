<p><ul><li>Vapor Compression Refrigeration</li><li>Absorption Refrigeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Central air-conditioning systems are commonly classified into two main types based on the refrigeration technology used: Vapor Compression Refrigeration and Absorption Refrigeration. </p><p>Vapor Compression Refrigeration systems are the most commonly used type, which operate by compressing and expanding a refrigerant gas to remove heat from the air. </p><p>On the other hand, Absorption Refrigeration systems utilize a combination of a refrigerant gas and a liquid absorbent to achieve cooling. These systems are commonly used in industrial and commercial settings due to their energy efficiency and environmental friendliness.</p></p>
