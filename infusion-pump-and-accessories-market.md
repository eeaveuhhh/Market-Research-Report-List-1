<p><p>Infusion pumps are medical devices used to deliver fluids, such as nutrients or medications, into a patient's body in a controlled manner. The market for infusion pumps is divided into different types based on their intended use. </p><p>Volumetric infusion pumps are used to deliver fluids at a specific rate over time. Syringe pumps use a syringe to deliver small amounts of fluid. Feeding pumps are specifically designed for enteral feeding. Insulin pumps are used to deliver insulin to diabetic patients. PCA pumps are used for patient-controlled analgesia, allowing patients to control their own pain medication delivery.</p></p>
