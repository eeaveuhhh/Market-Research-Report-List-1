<p><p>Arc welding torches are essential tools in the welding industry, and they come in two main types: air-cooled welding torches and water-cooled welding torches. Air-cooled torches are lighter and more portable, making them ideal for smaller jobs or when maneuverability is important. On the other hand, water-cooled torches are designed for heavy-duty and high-temperature applications, as they can handle more heat without overheating. Both types of torches offer unique advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs of the welding project.</p></p>
