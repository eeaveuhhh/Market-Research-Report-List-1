<p><p>Multiaxis CNC machines are categorized based on the number of axes they can operate on simultaneously. 2-Axis CNC machines are able to operate on two axes, typically X and Y. 3-Axis CNC machines can operate on three axes, adding the Z axis for more complex cuts. 4-Axis CNC machines add a rotational axis, usually A or B, allowing for more intricate cuts and designs. 5-Axis CNC machines are the most advanced, with the ability to move along five axes simultaneously, offering unparalleled precision and versatility in machining operations.</p></p>
