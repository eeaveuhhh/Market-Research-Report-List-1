<p><p>Feed preservatives are essential additives in animal feed to maintain its quality and prevent spoilage. There are four main types of feed preservatives: feed acidifiers, mold inhibitors, feed antioxidants, and anticaking agents. Feed acidifiers help in controlling pH levels, mold inhibitors prevent the growth of mold and bacteria, antioxidants protect feed from oxidation, and anticaking agents prevent clumping. Each type of preservative serves a specific purpose in ensuring the nutritional integrity and safety of animal feed.</p></p>
