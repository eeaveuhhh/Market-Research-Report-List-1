<p><p>Epoxy-based instant adhesives can be classified into two main types: Cold cure adhesive and Heat cure adhesive. Cold cure adhesive refers to adhesives that harden at room temperature or when exposed to air, making them suitable for quick and easy bonding applications. Heat cure adhesive, on the other hand, requires heat to initiate the curing process, resulting in a stronger bond that can withstand higher temperatures. Both types offer fast bonding solutions for a variety of materials and applications.</p></p>
