<p><p>Double-Clad Fiber Laser market includes both Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser types. Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser operates continuously, emitting a constant laser beam, making it suitable for applications requiring steady output, such as cutting and welding. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Laser generates short bursts of high-intensity laser beams, making it ideal for tasks like marking and engraving. Both types offer unique advantages, catering to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
