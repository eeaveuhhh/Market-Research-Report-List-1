<p><p>Timber decking boards can be broadly classified into two main types: hardwood boards and softwood boards. Hardwood boards are known for their durability, resistance to rot and insect damage, and overall longevity. They are ideal for high-traffic areas and areas with harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, softwood boards are more affordable, easier to work with, and can be stained or painted to achieve the desired look. They are suitable for areas with moderate usage and milder weather conditions. Both types of boards offer unique benefits and cater to different preferences and budgets.</p></p>
