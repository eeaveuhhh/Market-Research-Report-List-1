<p><p>Feed Microecologics market consists of three main types: Probiotics, Prebiotics, and Synbiotics. Probiotics are live bacteria that promote good gut health in animals. Prebiotics are non-digestible fibers that promote the growth of beneficial bacteria in the gut. Synbiotics are a combination of probiotics and prebiotics, working together to enhance the overall health of the gut. These market types play a crucial role in improving digestion, immunity, and overall animal health in the feed industry.</p></p>
