<p><p>Inkjet inks are classified into different types based on their composition: water-based, oil-based, and solvent-based. Water-based inks are widely used for their eco-friendly nature and are suitable for printing on various materials. Oil-based inks are known for their durability and resistance to smudging, making them ideal for high-quality printing. Solvent-based inks are versatile and can adhere to a wide range of surfaces, making them popular for large-scale commercial printing. Each type of ink has its unique properties and applications in the market.</p></p>
