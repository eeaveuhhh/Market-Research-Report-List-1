<p><strong>The Submerged Arc Welded Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LSAW Pipe</li><li>SSAW Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submerged Arc Welded Pipe Market Types include LSAW Pipe (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded Pipe) and SSAW Pipe (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded Pipe). LSAW Pipe is manufactured by bending and shaping steel plates while SSAW Pipe is produced by spirally bending the steel coil and welding it together. LSAW Pipes are commonly used for oil and gas transmission pipelines, while SSAW Pipes are ideal for construction projects, water transmission, and oil and gas transportation due to their high strength and stability.</p></p>
