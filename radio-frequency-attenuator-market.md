<p><p>Radio Frequency attenuators come in three main types: Step Attenuator, Continuous Variable Attenuator, and Programmable Attenuator. Step Attenuators allow for discrete adjustment of signal levels in predefined steps. Continuous Variable Attenuators offer seamless adjustment of signal levels within a specified range. Programmable Attenuators provide the flexibility to adjust signal levels remotely or automatically using pre-programmed settings. Each type caters to different requirements in various RF applications, providing precise control over signal levels.</p></p>
