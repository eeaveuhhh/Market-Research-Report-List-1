<p><p>Needle-free connectors allow the administration of fluids without the use of needles, reducing the risk of needle-stick injuries and contamination. Positive pressure connectors deliver fluid under pressure, preventing backflow. Negative pressure connectors use suction to withdraw fluids from the line. Neutral pressure connectors maintain equilibrium between the line and the catheter. Each type has its own advantages and uses in different medical settings. The market for needle-free connectors continues to expand as healthcare facilities prioritize the safety and convenience of both patients and healthcare workers.</p></p>
