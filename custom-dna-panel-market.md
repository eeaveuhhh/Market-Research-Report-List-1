<p><strong>The Custom DNA Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amplicon-based approach in custom DNA panel market involves amplifying specific target regions of DNA for analysis. This method is cost-effective and efficient for focused genetic testing. On the other hand, hybridization-based approach uses probes to target and capture specific DNA sequences for analysis. This method allows for comprehensive coverage of a wide range of genes or regions of interest. Both approaches offer flexibility in customizing DNA panels for various research or diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
