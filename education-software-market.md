<p><p>Education software market can be classified into two main types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises software is installed and operated from the user's own local servers and infrastructure, providing a higher level of control and security but requiring more maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering greater flexibility and scalability with lower upfront costs. Both types cater to the unique needs and preferences of educational institutions seeking to enhance their learning and administrative processes.</p></p>
