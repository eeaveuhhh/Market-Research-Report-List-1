<p><p>The nerve repair market comprises various types of products such as nerve wraps, nerve protectors, nerve connectors, and nerve grafts. Nerve wraps are used to provide mechanical support and protection to the damaged nerves. Nerve protectors serve as barriers to prevent the infiltration of scar tissue. Nerve connectors help in bridging the gap between severed nerves, allowing them to reestablish connections. Nerve grafts involve the transplantation of a nerve segment from another location in the body to restore nerve continuity. These products play a crucial role in promoting nerve regeneration and functional recovery.</p></p>
