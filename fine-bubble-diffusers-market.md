<p><ul><li>Disc Diffusers</li><li>Tube Diffusers</li><li>Plate Diffusers</li><li>Dome Diffusers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fine bubble diffusers are used in wastewater treatment to aerate and mix water. They come in various types including disc diffusers, tube diffusers, plate diffusers, and dome diffusers. Disc diffusers have a high oxygen transfer efficiency and low energy consumption. Tube diffusers provide uniform bubble distribution and are easy to maintain. Plate diffusers are durable and have a long service life. Dome diffusers are suitable for deep tanks and have a large surface area for oxygen transfer. Each type has its own unique benefits and applications in the market.</p></p>
