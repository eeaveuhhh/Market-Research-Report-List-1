<p><p>Inorganic matting agents are available in powder and gel forms for use in various industries. Powder matting agents are finely ground particles that are easily dispersed in coatings and paints to create a dull, matte appearance. Gel matting agents are thixotropic materials that offer better control over gloss levels by forming a gel-like structure in the coating. Both types of matting agents provide excellent scratch and abrasion resistance, making them ideal for applications where durability is essential.</p></p>
