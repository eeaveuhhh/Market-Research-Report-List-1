<p><ul><li>Straight Body Spotting Scope</li><li>Angled Body Spotting Scope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spotting scope market is categorized into two main types: straight body spotting scopes and angled body spotting scopes. Straight body spotting scopes have a straight barrel design, offering a more ergonomic viewing experience when observing subjects at eye level or at lower heights. On the other hand, angled body spotting scopes feature an angled eyepiece, providing a more comfortable viewing position when observing subjects at higher elevations or when sharing the scope among multiple users. These two types cater to different preferences and intended uses, allowing users to choose the design that suits their needs best.</p></p>
