<p><p>Dry Strength Agents are chemicals added to paper during the paper manufacturing process to increase its strength and quality. There are three main types of Dry Strength Agents: Polyvinylamine (PVAm), GPAM (Glyoxylated Polyacrylamide), and Starch-based Polymers. PVAm is known for its high efficiency and stability, while GPAM offers excellent wet and dry strength properties. Starch-based Polymers are biodegradable and environmentally friendly options for improving paper strength. These agents play a crucial role in enhancing the overall quality and performance of paper products.</p></p>
