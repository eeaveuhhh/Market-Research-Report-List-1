<p><p>Chemical Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) involves the use of chemicals to increase the amount of crude oil that can be extracted from reservoirs. There are three main types of chemicals used in EOR: Petro-based, Bio-based, and Water-based. Petro-based chemicals are derived from petroleum sources and are commonly used in traditional EOR methods. Bio-based chemicals are derived from renewable resources and are considered more environmentally friendly. Water-based chemicals are typically used in combination with other chemicals to improve oil recovery efficiency. Each type of chemical has unique properties and benefits for EOR operations.</p></p>
