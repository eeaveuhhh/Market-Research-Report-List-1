<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCB) are used in various electronic devices and come in different types to suit different requirements. Single Panel boards are the most basic and cost-effective, while Double Panel boards have circuits on both sides. Four Layer Boards have circuits on four layers, allowing for more complex designs. Six Layer Boards offer even more flexibility for intricate designs. Other markets include flexible PCBs for bent or twisted applications, and Rigid-Flex PCBs that combine rigid and flexible boards for versatile applications.</p></p>
