<p><p>Bicycle rear shock absorbers come in two main types: air shock absorbers and coil shock absorbers. Air shock absorbers use compressed air to absorb shock and provide a more adjustable and lightweight option for riders. Coil shock absorbers, on the other hand, use a coil spring to absorb impact and are known for their durability and reliability. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices among cyclists depending on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
