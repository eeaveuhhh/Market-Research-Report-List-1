<p><p>The small molecule API (active pharmaceutical ingredient) market consists of two main types: synthetic & chemical API and biological API. Synthetic & chemical APIs are traditionally produced through chemical synthesis and are typically small molecules, allowing for easy absorption and distribution within the body. On the other hand, biological APIs are derived from living sources such as proteins or nucleic acids. These APIs are usually larger molecules, requiring advanced manufacturing techniques like biotechnology or genetic engineering. Both types play a crucial role in the development of various pharmaceutical drugs.</p></p>
