<p><p>Premixed plasters come in two main types: dry premixed plasters and wet premixed plasters. Dry premixed plasters are powders that only require mixing with water before application. They are convenient to store and transport. Wet premixed plasters are ready-to-use products that eliminate the need for any additional mixing. They are ideal for quick projects and are often used for small repairs or touch-ups. Both types cater to different preferences and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
