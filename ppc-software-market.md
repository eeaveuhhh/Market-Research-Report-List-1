<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PPC software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based PPC software operates on remote servers, enabling users to access and manage their PPC campaigns through internet-connected devices. This type offers advantages like flexibility, scalability, and minimal hardware requirements. On the other hand, on-premises PPC software is installed locally on the user's own servers or computers, providing greater control over data and security. However, it requires maintenance and may have limited accessibility. Both types have their own benefits, and businesses choose based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
