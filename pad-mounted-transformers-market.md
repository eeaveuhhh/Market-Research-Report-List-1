<p><p>Pad-Mounted Transformers (PMTs) are electrical power distribution transformers that are designed for installation on a concrete pad or an underground vault. These transformers are commonly used in urban areas where space is limited, and they provide a secure and compact solution for step-down voltage distribution from medium voltage to low voltage. PMTs are mounted closer to the load, ensuring efficient and reliable power distribution.</p><p>The Pad-Mounted Transformers Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for reliable and affordable power supply, especially in developing regions, is driving the market growth. These transformers play a crucial role in integrating renewable energy systems into the power grid by stepping down the voltage for local distribution.</p><p>The market is also experiencing various trends that are shaping its growth.
