<p><p>Pre-Oriented Yarn, also known as POY, is a type of synthetic yarn that is stretched and oriented before being wound onto a bobbin. This process aligns the molecular chain of the yarn and increases its strength and elasticity, making it ideal for various applications such as textiles, automotive, electronics, and industrial uses. POY is commonly used in the production of fabrics, ropes, belts, and hoses due to its superior properties.</p><p>The Pre-Oriented Yarn Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period. This trend is expected to drive innovation and growth in the POY market in the coming years.</p></p>
