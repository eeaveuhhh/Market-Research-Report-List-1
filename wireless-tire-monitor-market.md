<p><p>The Wireless Tire Monitor market consists of two main types: built-in and external monitors. On the other hand, external monitors are placed on the valve stem and are a more cost-effective option. Built-in monitors offer a more seamless and convenient solution, while external monitors are easier to install and maintain.
