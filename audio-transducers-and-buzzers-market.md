<p><p>Audio transducers and buzzers are devices used to convert electrical signals into audible sound. The electromagnetic market utilizes a coil of wire and a vibrating diaphragm to produce sound waves. On the other hand, the piezoelectric market uses a ceramic material that changes shape when an electrical current is applied, creating sound waves. Both types of transducers are commonly used in alarms, timers, and notifications in various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
