<p><p>Non-Contact Phase Rotation Meter and Non-Contact Phase Sequence Meter are two types of market indicators used for electrical testing. The Non-Contact Phase Rotation Meter is designed to determine the phase sequence of a three-phase power source without making contact with the conductor, ensuring safe and accurate testing. On the other hand, the Non-Contact Phase Sequence Meter is used to verify the correct sequence of phase rotation in a three-phase system, helping to prevent potential damage to equipment and ensuring efficient power distribution.</p></p>
