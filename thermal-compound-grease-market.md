<p><p>Thermal compounds and greases are used to improve the heat transfer between electronic components and heat sinks. The three main types are: metal-based, ceramic-based, and carbon-based. Metal-based compounds have high thermal conductivity and are often used in high-performance applications. Ceramic-based compounds are non-electrically conductive and are ideal for use in delicate electronic components. Carbon-based compounds offer good thermal conductivity and are often more cost-effective compared to metal-based compounds. Each type caters to different requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
