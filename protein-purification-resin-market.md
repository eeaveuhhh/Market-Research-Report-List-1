<p><p>Protein purification resin is segmented into three main types: Protein A Purification Resin, Protein G Purification Resin, and Protein L Purification Resin. Protein A Purification Resin is widely used due to its high specificity and affinity for antibodies, making it ideal for monoclonal antibody purification. Protein G Purification Resin is another popular choice for antibody purification, offering binding to a wide range of antibody subclasses. Protein L Purification Resin is known for binding to antibody light chains, making it a valuable tool for purifying antibodies lacking Fc regions.</p></p>
