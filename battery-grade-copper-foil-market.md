<p><strong>The Battery Grade Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Below 6μm</li><li>6μm-8μm</li><li>More than 8μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Battery-grade copper foil is classified into three main types based on thickness: below 6μm, 6μm-8μm, and more than 8μm. Below 6μm copper foil is the thinnest and is often used in ultra-thin and lightweight batteries. 6μm-8μm copper foil is commonly used in mainstream lithium-ion batteries. More than 8μm copper foil is thicker and is used in high-capacity batteries that require increased durability and conductivity. Each type of copper foil serves different purposes in the battery manufacturing industry.</p></p>
