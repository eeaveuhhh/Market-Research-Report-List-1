<p><strong>The Sunflower Oilseed Processing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Chemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sunflower oilseed processing involves two main markets: mechanical and chemical. Mechanical processing involves processes like pressing, crushing, and extracting oil from the seeds using machinery. It is a more traditional and labor-intensive method. Chemical processing, on the other hand, involves using solvents like hexane to extract oil from the seeds, which is a more modern and efficient method. Both markets cater to the demand for sunflower oil products, with mechanical processing being favored for its natural and organic qualities, while chemical processing is preferred for its speed and higher oil yield.</p></p>
