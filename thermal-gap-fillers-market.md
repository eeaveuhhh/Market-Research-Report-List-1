<p><p>Thermal gap fillers are materials used to fill gaps between surfaces to improve thermal conductivity and reduce heat buildup. There are two types of gap fillers in the market: sheet gap filling materials and liquid gap filling materials. Sheet gap fillers are pre-cut sheets of material that can be easily placed between surfaces, while liquid gap fillers are pourable materials that conform to the shape of the gap. Both types of gap fillers are essential in various industries to enhance thermal management and improve overall performance.</p></p>
